About this place

Welcome to this beautifully fully renovated two-bedroom home in the heart of downtown Windsor. Featuring high-end Gus Modern furniture, granite kitchen and laundry countertops, and two queen-sized mattresses with luxury high thread-count sheets — everything here is thoughtfully chosen for your comfort.

Conveniently located just a few blocks from the Windsor-Detroit Tunnel, and within walking distance to Caesar's Windsor Casino, the scenic Detroit River waterfront, and Little Italy on Erie Street. A private backyard and one-car garage make this the perfect home base for any trip to Windsor.

The home is modernly designed with a fully stocked kitchen, high-speed Wi-Fi, and HDTV with Netflix via Chromecast. We pride ourselves on our cleanliness — everything is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before your arrival. Guest has access to the entire house.
