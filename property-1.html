About this place

Come and stay at this 5-star inspired home! Over 3,400 SQ FT of living space featuring high-end furniture, a King Bed with Beautyrest Harmony Lux Mattress, Hotel Collection towels, and high thread count sheets.

The beautiful open concept space boasts 12-foot high ceilings throughout the entire unit with main level laundry. Enjoy 2 living rooms, 6 flatscreen Sony & Samsung TVs, a fully stocked kitchen, and a Keurig Coffee & Tea Bar.

Unwind in the private gym featuring a treadmill, free weights, bench, yoga mats, and a sauna. Stay entertained with board games, ping pong, and an arcade game room. Streaming includes Netflix, Disney Plus, Prime, and Cable TV. We provide premium toiletries including organic Crate 61 Soap and Chi Brand shampoo. Guest has access to the entire property!
